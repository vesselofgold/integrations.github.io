Integration Delivery Service Team

Integration Delivery Service Team (IDST) will be comprised of the following roles:

Product Owner Duties Provide oversight, direction and leadership for integration services

Technical BA Duties Translate business requirements into technical requirements, & act as curator for integration services information and processes; be the one responsible for the new ISB Services/Resources Portal and associated documentation

Integration Specialists (SRE) Duties Act as central coordinator for integrations working with sector stakeholders. Work with internal and external developers on solutions

Scrum/Agilist IS27 Duties Provide scrum and agile coaching and education to ISB, second, be the scrum master and agile coach for the SRE dev ops teams
