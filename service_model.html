Service Model

Integration Delivery Service Team will act as the sector’s central coordinator for all things related to integrations!

HYBRID SERVICE – CURRENTLY OFFERED

Integration delivery services team provides guidance, information, tools and processes to business areas and their vendor development teams.  In this service, the integration delivery services team may or may not contribute to the integration development work.  Business areas are expected to facilitate technical development.

FULL SERVICE – FUTURE STATE

Integration delivery services team providing the development and the delivery of integration work.  This is not available until the integration delivery service team is staffed.
