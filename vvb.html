Vision, Value and Benefits

Organizations practicing good integration create an agile integrated digital ecosystem and gain:

  • Agility to keep critical business and financial information up-to-date and make better, faster business decisions
  • Visibility into the data flowing throughout your business so you can proactively manage processes and troubleshoot errors
  • Efficiency by connecting disparate applications and improving processes across the enterprise
  • Time and cost savings by eliminating manual processes and costly legacy systems, so IT resources can focus on strategic activities
  • Accelerated growth  with the ability to bring on new business – and gain revenue – faster with out-of-the-box solutions that are easy to deploy

Future value/benefits:

  1. Allows us to integrate with partners or stakeholders
  2. Allow us to track and analyze a wide range of data (product/process improvements)
  3. Allows us decrease costs with better business or technology processes
  4. Allows us to break down silos between systems or business areas
  5. Modernizing ISB’s approach to service delivery
